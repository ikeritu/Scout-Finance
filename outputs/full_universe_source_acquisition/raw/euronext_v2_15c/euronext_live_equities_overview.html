    Best of Book
    Euronext’s Best of Book (BoB) is a Best Execution service exclusively for retail investors.

    It provides the best prices available in Europe for their equity orders through the main European stock exchange thanks to dedicated liquidity providers.


    Are you looking for Best Execution?
    Ask your broker to send your orders to Euronext’s Best of Book

    Best of Book is available for stocks through the following Euronext regulated markets: Amsterdam, Brussels, Dublin, Lisbon, Milan, Oslo and Paris.

    Learn more about Best of Book

    Are you a broker and interested in offering Best of Book to your clients? Visit our trading section on Euronext.com

    Trade non-Euronext listed European and US stocks

    • Meets MiFID II best execution requirements and 
    • Get on-book liquidity thanks to the support of market makers
    • Trade a broader range of pan-European (Germany, Spain, Finland, Sweden, Austria, on top of Euronext countries) and US stocks
    • Benefit from clearing and settlement in Euros, respectively by Euronext Clearing and Euronext Securities Milan
